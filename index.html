How to play:

Use the arrow keys or the buttons below to change the direction of the snake.

If the snakes eats an apple, it will grow and speed up. One point will be added to your score.

If the snakes eats a pear, it will grow and speed up faster than after eating an apple. Your score will increase by 2 points in this case.

Don't hit the walls or let the snake eat itself!

Goal: Get as many points as possible!

Have fun! ✨
